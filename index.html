● FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer free delivery in Hampshire?

Yes! JB Furniture offers free delivery across Hampshire on all orders. We provide a white-glove delivery service where our team will carefully place your furniture exactly where you want it in your home.

What is Staingard Protect6 and is it really free?

Staingard Protect6 is a comprehensive furniture protection plan that covers accidental damage for 5 years, including stains, rips, tears, and burns. At JB Furniture, we include this FREE with every sofa purchase — a benefit worth over £100 that gives you complete peace of mind.

Do you offer finance on furniture purchases?

Yes, we offer flexible finance options on all our furniture, starting from just £29 per month. We have 0% interest available on selected items, subject to status. Apply in-store and get an instant decision.

Are your beds UK-made?

Yes! Our bed range is manufactured by Highgrove Beds in Liversedge, Yorkshire. Every mattress is handcrafted in the UK using premium materials, ensuring exceptional quality and supporting British manufacturing.

Can I visit your showroom in Aldershot?

Absolutely! Our showroom at 31-33 Victoria Road, Wellington Centre, Aldershot GU11 1DB is open Monday to Saturday 9:30am-5:30pm and Sunday 10:30am-4:00pm. Come and experience our furniture in person — no appointment needed.

What brands do you stock?

We are official stockists of Highgrove Beds, Vida Living, Meta Sofa, Sofa Source, Maysons (AC Furniture), Loren Williams, and Staingard. Every brand is selected for quality, durability, and design.
